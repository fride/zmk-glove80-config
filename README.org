* Ein Keyboard, mein Keybaord. Hoffentlich ein gutes.

I need a layout for
- typing in english and german
- for coding, meaning symbols and shortcuts must be easy to reach
- gamingin is out of scope. I just use another keyboard.

    v g l þ *  * u o , z

    x f w d b  j y ' . p
            r  ⎵
    #+END_SRC
